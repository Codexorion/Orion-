Key Tech Crises in Cameroon

  • Internet shutdowns: Lost revenue, education disruption
  • Cybercrime: Financial fraud, reduced trust
  • Poor infrastructure: Expensive, slow internet
  • Brain drain: Shortage of skilled talent
  • Weak startup ecosystem: Limited innovation

Student Action Plan

  1. Form tech clubs (coding, robotics, cybersecurity)
  2. Learn practical skills: coding, cloud, AI, cybersecurity
  3. Develop local solutions: apps, awareness campaigns
  4. Improve campus connectivity (Wi-Fi projects, intranet)
  5. Promote digital rights and internet freedom
  6. Participate in hackathons and startups
  7. Collaborate with industry and mentors
  8. Fight brain drain by remote work for local benefit
